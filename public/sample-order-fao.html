About This Case Type (FAO): A First Appeal from Order (FAO) is an appeal filed against an interim order of a lower court. Unlike an appeal from a final judgment, an FAO challenges a specific, intermediate order passed during the pendency of a case (e.g., an order granting or refusing a temporary injunction). It allows parties to get quick relief from potentially incorrect interim decisions without waiting for the entire case to conclude.
