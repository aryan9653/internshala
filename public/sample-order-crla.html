Understanding a Criminal Appeal (CRL.A)

A Criminal Appeal is a request made to a higher court (like the High Court) to review and change the decision of a lower court (like a Sessions Court) in a criminal case. This can be filed by the person convicted, seeking acquittal or a reduced sentence, or by the State, seeking a conviction or an enhanced sentence. In this instance, it is an appeal against a conviction for murder (Section 302 of the Indian Penal Code).
