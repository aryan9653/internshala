About This Case Type

A Civil Suit (Original Side) or CS(OS) is a lawsuit of a civil nature filed directly in the High Court, which exercises its "Original Jurisdiction" to hear the case like a trial court would. This typically happens for suits of high pecuniary value (monetary value exceeding a certain threshold) or specific types of cases like intellectual property disputes. The High Court hears evidence, examines witnesses, and passes a final judgment. This mock case is a commercial dispute for the recovery of money.
