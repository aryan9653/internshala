Understanding a Civil Suit (Original Side) - CS(OS)

A Civil Suit (Original Side) is a lawsuit of a civil nature that originates directly in the High Court, bypassing the lower district courts. This typically happens when the monetary value of the dispute is very high (above a certain threshold set by law) or involves specific matters that the High Court is empowered to hear first. These suits deal with non-criminal disputes like property, contracts, and monetary claims.
