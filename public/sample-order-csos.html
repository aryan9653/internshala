About Civil Suit (Original Side) - CS(OS)

A Civil Suit (Original Side) refers to a lawsuit filed directly in the High Court, which is acting as a court of first instance rather than an appellate court. The 'Original Jurisdiction' of High Courts is typically limited to specific matters, such as those of high pecuniary value, intellectual property disputes, or as specified by statute. This particular suit is for the recovery of money and damages, indicating a commercial dispute where the financial stakes are high enough to warrant initial proceedings in the High Court.
