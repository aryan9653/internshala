Understanding Civil Suits (Original Side) - CS(OS)

A Civil Suit (Original Side) or CS(OS) is a lawsuit of a civil nature that originates directly in the High Court, bypassing the lower district courts. High Courts like Delhi have "Original Pecuniary Jurisdiction," meaning they can hear fresh cases if the financial value of the dispute is above a certain threshold (e.g., above â‚¹2 Crore).
