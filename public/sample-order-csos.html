Understanding a Civil Suit (Original Side) - CS(OS)

A Civil Suit (Original Side) means the High Court is hearing the case for the first time, similar to a trial court. This happens for civil matters of high pecuniary (monetary) value. In Delhi, suits where the value of the dispute is over a certain threshold (e.g., â‚¹2 crore) are filed directly in the High Court. 'Original Jurisdiction' is the power to hear a case from its inception, as opposed to 'Appellate Jurisdiction,' which is the power to review a lower court's decision. These suits cover disputes related to contracts, property, intellectual property rights, etc.

Application for summary judgment dismissed

Type: order | Date: 15-07-2024

The application filed by the Plaintiff under Order XIIIA for summary judgment is dismissed. The court is of the view that there are triable issues that require leading of evidence. The case will now proceed to trial as per the regular procedure.

Purpose of the Order: A summary judgment is a request to decide the case without a full trial, arguing that the other party has no real prospect of succeeding. By dismissing this application, the court has decided that the defendant's case has enough merit to warrant a full trial where evidence will be presented and witnesses examined. This is a setback for the plaintiff who hoped for a quick resolution.
