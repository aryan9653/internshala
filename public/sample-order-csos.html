Understanding Civil Suits (Original Side) - CS(OS)

A Civil Suit (Original Side) or CS(OS) is a lawsuit of a civil nature filed directly in the High Court, bypassing the lower district courts. This happens when the monetary value of the dispute is very high (above a certain threshold set by law). These cases often involve complex commercial disputes, intellectual property rights, or significant property matters.
