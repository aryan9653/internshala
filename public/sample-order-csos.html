What is a Civil Suit (Original Side) - CS(OS)?

A Civil Suit (Original Side) refers to a lawsuit of a civil nature that is filed directly in a High Court, bypassing the lower district courts. High Courts in major cities like Delhi, Mumbai, and Chennai have "Original Jurisdiction," which allows them to hear such cases from the very beginning (the "original" stage) if the financial value of the dispute is above a certain threshold. These suits typically involve commercial disputes, property matters, intellectual property rights, and other high-stake civil conflicts. The "OS" stands for "Original Side."
