Understanding a Civil Suit (Original Side) - CS(OS)

A Civil Suit (Original Side) or CS(OS) is a lawsuit of a civil nature that is filed directly in the High Court, bypassing the lower district courts. The High Court hears the case as the court of first instance. This typically happens for cases of high pecuniary value (monetary value of the dispute) or specific types of cases that are designated to be heard by the High Court directly, such as intellectual property disputes.
