About This Case Type

A Writ Petition (Civil) is a petition filed before a High Court or the Supreme Court for the enforcement of Fundamental Rights guaranteed by the Constitution of India. It is a powerful legal tool used to command a public authority (like the government) to perform its duties or to prevent it from acting unlawfully. In this mock case, it's a Public Interest Litigation (PIL) concerning environmental clearances, where the petitioner seeks the court's intervention to protect the environment.
