Understanding a Writ Petition (Civil) - W.P.(C)

A Writ Petition (Civil) is a formal written order from a higher court (like the High Court or Supreme Court) to a lower court or a government entity. It is a powerful legal tool used to enforce fundamental rights guaranteed by the Constitution of India. Citizens can file a writ petition if they believe their fundamental rights have been violated by the state or a government authority. It is often used in matters of public interest, such as environmental protection, public health, and administrative fairness.
