Understanding Writ Petitions (Civil) - W.P.(C)

A Writ Petition (Civil) is a powerful legal tool used by citizens to enforce their fundamental rights against the State (i.e., government bodies and authorities). When a person believes a government action or inaction has violated their rights, they can directly approach the High Court.
