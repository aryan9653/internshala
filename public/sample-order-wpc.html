Understanding W.P.(C) - Writ Petition (Civil)

A Writ Petition (Civil) is filed under Article 226 of the Constitution of India. It is a powerful legal tool used to protect the fundamental rights of citizens. Individuals or groups can approach the High Court to seek remedies against the actions of government bodies, public officials, or other state instrumentalities. A "Public Interest Litigation" (PIL) is a type of writ petition where the case is filed not for personal gain but for the benefit of the public at large, often concerning issues like environmental protection, human rights, or public health.
