Understanding Writ Petitions (Civil) - W.P.(C)

A Writ Petition (Civil) is a powerful legal tool used to enforce fundamental rights guaranteed by the Constitution of India. Citizens can directly approach the High Court (under Article 226) or the Supreme Court (under Article 32) if their rights are violated by the state or a government body. In this case, it is a Public Interest Litigation (PIL), where a citizen or organization raises issues of public concern—here, environmental protection—on behalf of the wider community.
