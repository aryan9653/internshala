Understanding a Writ Petition (Civil) - W.P.(C)

A Writ Petition is a formal request to a High Court to intervene when a person's fundamental rights have been violated. The "Civil" part means it deals with non-criminal matters. Writs are a powerful tool used to command government authorities to perform their duties or to correct an injustice. This specific case is a Public Interest Litigation (PIL), where a citizen or group approaches the court on behalf of the public for a larger cause.
