What is a Writ Petition (Civil) - W.P.(C)?

A Writ Petition (Civil) is a formal written order from a higher court (like the High Court or Supreme Court) to a lower court or a government entity. It is a powerful tool under the Indian Constitution used to enforce fundamental rights. In a W.P.(C), a person or entity (the "petitioner") asks the court to intervene when they believe a public authority has violated their rights or failed to perform its duties. This specific case is a Public Interest Litigation (PIL), where the petition is filed for the benefit of the public at large, rather than for an individual grievance.
